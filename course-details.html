ABOUT US

Diploma in Local Governance & TQM

1. Background

Ours is an era of survival of the fittest in which competency building is inevitable. In view of this, UGC is taking steps for fostering quality in higher education. UGC insists outcome based education system for the students to excel in multiple domains, which is the need of the time. Strategies for this include outreach programmes, industry exposure, add-on courses, etc. The Diploma, the add-on courses to PG in Commerce or Management or Social Science, is a leap towards this direction by KIED. Diploma in Local Governance and TQM (DLG-TQM), first of its kind, is announced by KIED in the context that the Local Governments (LGs) in Kerala are towards the path of improving performance through the application of management tool viz. TQM. Implementing Total Quality Management (TQM) to improve the service delivery mechanism and availing ISO 9001: 2015 is one of the good practices of LGs in Kerala. Being the students of commerce or management or social science, do we have any idea how such management tools are applied in the field? How the theory is put into practice? The DLG-TQM for LGs provides you exposure to such real field situation. Enhancing your domain would also open the scope for future career prospects in LGs, it is hoped.

2. Purpose and Objectives

The purpose of this Diploma is to build professionals with knowledge and skills in effective application of TQM for Local Governments. To attain this purpose, this Diploma carries the objectives of equipping the PG students to:

i. explain the local governance system in Kerala,
ii. explain the concepts and components of TQM,
iii. apply TQM tools,
iv. explain the history and requirements of ISO 9001: 2015, and
v. do the ISO 9001: 2015 certification process.

3. Participants

The Post Graduate students of Commerce, Management, Social Science, and Engineering discipline who are aspiring to build their career in local governance.
